    Planning and development

    The processing of planning applications, building control, and local spatial planning.

    About this area

    Planning and development sits in the Business Areas layer of the Local Government Architecture Model. It covers the statutory functions that councils perform around land use, building safety, and spatial planning — from processing individual planning applications through to maintaining the local plan.

    The functions within this area are defined by the LGA taxonomy under "Planning and building control" (function 99). Each function contains a set of granular services that councils deliver. This page also links to the open planning datasets published by MHCLG and the LGAM capabilities that support planning services.
